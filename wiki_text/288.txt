Waterfall in Odisha, IndiaBadaghagara Waterfallବଡ଼ଘାଗରା ଜଳପ୍ରପାତBadaghagara Waterfall, KendujharLocationKendujhar district, Odisha, IndiaCoordinates21°36′32″N 85°33′21″E﻿ / ﻿21.6089°N 85.5557°E﻿ / 21.6089; 85.5557Total height60 metres (200 ft)Number of drops1WatercourseMachha Kandana
The Badaghagara Waterfall is a waterfall of the Kendujhar district in the Indian state of Odisha.


Location[edit]
Badaghagara is located at a distance of 9 km from the district headquarters (Kendujhar) of Kendujhar district. Being a perennial source of water, a dam has constructed on the downstream side. It is situated at a distance of 3 kilometers on the downstream of Sanaghagara Waterfall.

The falls[edit]
It is a perennial waterfall. The Machha Kandana, a small river, plunges from a height of 60 metres (200 ft) in a single drop.
