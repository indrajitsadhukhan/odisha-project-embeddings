Labesvara Siva TempleReligionAffiliationHinduismDeitySivaLocationLocationBhubaneswarStateOrissaCountryIndiaLocation in OrissaGeographic coordinates20°15′22″N 85°50′18″E﻿ / ﻿20.25611°N 85.83833°E﻿ / 20.25611; 85.83833Coordinates: 20°15′22″N 85°50′18″E﻿ / ﻿20.25611°N 85.83833°E﻿ / 20.25611; 85.83833ArchitectureTypeKalingan Style (Kalinga Architecture)Completed15th century A.D.Elevation26 m (85 ft)
Labesvara Siva Temple (Hanumantesvara) is a Hindu temple situated near Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. The sandstone temple shows signs of deterioration, including cracks in the roof through which rain water percolates into the sanctum.
