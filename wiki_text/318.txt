From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia



Balimela Wildlife Sanctuary is a wildlife sanctuary in Malkangiri district, Odisha, India.
It covers an area of 160 km². The terrain is hilly, and covered with mixed deciduous forests.[1] It is in the Eastern Highlands moist deciduous forests ecoregion.
