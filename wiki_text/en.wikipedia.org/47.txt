'''Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation''' ('''RTDC''') is an agency of the [[Government of Rajasthan]] set up on 1 April 1979 to develop [[Tourism in Rajasthan|tourism in the Indian state]].<ref name="राजस्थान_पर्यटन_">{{cite web|title=Rajasthan Tourism|url=http://www.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in/hi.html|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912092039/http://www.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in/hi.html|archive-date=12 September 2018|access-date=12 September 2018|website=tourism.rajasthan.gov.in|publisher=}}</ref> It manages many restaurants, cafeterias, hotels and bars. The Corporation also organises package tours, fairs, festivals, entertainment, shopping and transport services. In collaboration with [[Indian Railways]] it runs the [[Luxury trains|luxury tourist train]] [[Palace on Wheels]]. RTDC has hotels/motels at all major tourist places in [[Rajasthan]].<ref name="RTDC2018">{{cite web|title=Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation Limited|url=http://rtdc.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in/|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180911152043/http://rtdc.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in/|archive-date=11 September 2018|access-date=10 September 2018|website=rtdc.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in|publisher=}}</ref><ref name="rtdchotels">{{cite web|title=RTDC Hotels|url=http://rtdc.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in/Client/HotelList.aspx|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180911152056/http://rtdc.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in/Client/HotelList.aspx|archive-date=11 September 2018|access-date=11 September 2018|website=rtdc.tourism.rajasthan.gov.in|publisher=}}</ref>
